## Relationship to the main privacy policy

This addendum supplements the [MUULOX Privacy Policy (GDPR)](https://muulox.com/gdpr/). It describes the MUULOX mobile application (“the App”) only. For how MUULOX SRL processes personal data across products and services—including accounts, orders, payments, and the client portal—the **GDPR page prevails**. If anything in this addendum conflicts with the GDPR policy, the GDPR policy applies to personal data.

## What the App is

The App is a native launcher for MUULOX web services. It provides quick access to:

- the MUULOX online shop (`muulox.com`), and  
- the MUULOX client portal and related web tools.

Product listings, login, checkout, and portal features run **inside embedded web pages** (WebView). The App does not replace the website; it displays the same services you would use in a mobile browser.

## What the App processes (native layer only)

The App’s own code, as distributed today:

| Data | Use |
|------|-----|
| Device language | Used locally to show the App interface in English, Hungarian, or Romanian. Not sent to MUULOX servers by the App binary. |
| Internet connectivity | Required to load MUULOX websites. |
| URLs you open | Shop, portal, or (if unlocked) admin web addresses are loaded over HTTPS. |

The App does **not**, in its current version:

- maintain a separate MUULOX account database on the device beyond what the website stores in the browser session;  
- include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs;  
- store payment card details natively.

## What the website and portal handle

When you sign in, place orders, or use the portal inside the App, MUULOX processes data as described in the [GDPR policy](https://muulox.com/gdpr/) and related cookie notices on `muulox.com`. That may include, for example: account details, order and delivery data, payment information, portal usage, and cookies or similar technologies.

## WebView and shared devices

Sessions and cookies in the WebView behave like using the MUULOX site in a browser. On a shared phone or tablet, log out on the website when finished and avoid leaving accounts signed in.
